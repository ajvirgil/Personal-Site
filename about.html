BIOGRAPHY

About Me

General

Hi there. My name is AJ Virgil, thanks for checking out my site. I'm a full-time+ designer living & working in the beautiful state of Colorado. I'm passionate about creating work that is impactful, intuitive, & aesthetically pleasing. Check out my work here.

History

I currently serve at Axis, a 501(c)3 non-profit. I started in 2012 as a designer, and then in 2014, I transitioned to Creative Director where I now oversee brand, design, & web projects. I also interned at Lunchbucket Creative in 2012 & have been paidlancing since 2011.

Capabilities

I specialize in Creative Direction, Brand Identity Design, User Experience Design, User Interface Design, & Front End Development. I’m well versed in Adobe CC, Sketch, HTML, CSS/SASS, & Wordpress. I'm also learning new things like Javascript, Ruby, & Swift.

Outside the Studio

When I’m not pushing pixels or wrangling code I like to watch arthouse films, listen to vinyl records, drink tea, attempt to cook Vietnamese food, throw some yo-yo, read books, & write. I’m also a pretty serious minimalist; I only own about 200 things.

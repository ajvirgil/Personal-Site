ALL THE FACTS

About Me

General

I’m a full-stack designer living and working in the beautiful state of Colorado. I believe that design is not just a final product– but a process. It’s about solving problems by exploring the who, what, & why. I strive to solve these problems by delivering solutions that are beautiful, empathetic, & delightful to use.

History

I currently serve at Axis, a non-profit ministry. I joined Axis in 2012 as the lead Designer, and then later transitioned to Creative Director in 2014. At Axis I oversee all design, web, video, & photography projects. Before that I interned at Lunchbucket Creative. I’ve also been freelancing since 2011.

Capabilities

I specialize in Branding, User Experience Design, User Interface Design, & Front End Development. I’m well versed in Adobe CC, Sketch, HTML, CSS, & Wordpress. I'm also learning new things like Sass, Javascript, & Ruby.

Outside the Studio

When I’m not pushing pixels or wrangling code I like to watch arthouse films, listen to vinyl records, drink tea, attempt to cook Vietnamese food, throw some yo-yo, read books, & write. I’m also a pretty serious minimalist. I only own about 100 things.

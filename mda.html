BRANDING & WEB DESIGN

Michael D. Amos

Mike is a dear friend of mine, so I was honored when he asked me to help develop out an identity system for his personal brand. Mike is Ph.D candidate and trained Organizational Psychologist. He focuses on consulting mid-to-large sized companies in meaning based training and executive leadership coaching.

One of the main goals was to create a branding system that wholly reflected his passion and dedication to his work. Mike is confident go-getter, but he’s also warm and instantly likable. The objective was to design something that felt strong and assured, but also captured a feeling of friendliness.

As an other way to reinforce the idea of assertiveness was to incorporate the “Dash” or the underline below the “M”. This not only communicates importance, but it also creates a branding touchpoint that looks great at scale and is instantly memorable.

The end result is an identity system showcases Michael’s personality and values, but also accentuates a high level of professionalism and credibility as he continues to connect with and create value for his clients.
